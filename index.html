Hi there, I'm Kaikai Zhao.

I'm a first-year PhD student in the School of Informatics, Computing & Engineering, Indiana University, Bloomington. Before I came to IU, I spent one year and a half as a graduate student at National University of Defense Technology, China.


Research Interests

My research interests are on Machine Learning and Algorithms, especially on probablistic models and randomized algorithms. My research focuses on applying theory and algorithms to practical problems. Recently, my work explores variational inference and sparse Gaussian Processes.
