I write whatever stuff here!

For scientific researchers, I recommend Mendeley which is a free literature manager.

In terms of writing, I recommend Overleaf which is an online LaTeX editor. If you would like to save time on LaTeX commands, you can check out mathpix which can extract LaTeX from PDFs or handwritten notes in seconds.

If your research involves extensive mathematical derivations, Matrix Cookbook could be helpful.

I love reading, math and soccer. Also, I'm an avid reader. If you are a Chinese reader, I highly recommend Jun Wu's(吴军) books. Personally, you might benefit from reading his books.
